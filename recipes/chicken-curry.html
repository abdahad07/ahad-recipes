Chicken Curry and Rice

Description

This meal covers most of the macros needed throughout the day. Also can experiment with spices.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Rice
  • Stupid oil

Preparation

  1. Cook rice in pressure cooker
  2. Sautee onions till brown
  3. Add chicken and masala
  4. Put water and cover it for 10 mins
